Abstract: 

Kai-Fu Lee and Rick Shriner from Apple Computer will give a
presentation on the Apple Core Technology group and the Advanced
Technology Group on Thursday, January 26 from 12:00-1:30 pm in
Wean 4625. They will bring some of the latest Apple technologies
in communications, telephony, multimedia, and speech. Rick and Kai-Fu
invite interested grads and undergrads to attend this informal
demonstration and discussion.  Graduate students interested in
potential job opportunities are particularly encouraged to attend.
Pizza will be served.
